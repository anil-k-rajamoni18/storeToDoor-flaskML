About Us

Increased internet usage allowed many technological advancements that make the user or the customer have facilities to get the things they wish in just one click. Also, many businesses are making use of the Internet in their expansion and scaling.

The demand and supply chain of the customers and the business owners are synchronous with the development of the needs and want.

As everything is based on online communications, businesses are moving into e-commerce platforms. This increased the business and sales scaling.

In these e-commerce platforms, the owner and customer interaction is limited to promotional messages and product reviews. There is no scope for direct customer interactions. It needs an effective communication channel to make business transactions easier.

Also, the search preferences of the products, and pricing varies from person to person. Targeting customers who can prefer to choose certain products is the first strategy to implement. To make this easy, customer segmentation plays a major role.

When we researched about implementing this project, we found that the major problems include effective direct communications to understand customer preferences and reviews. The other problem is marketing to the right audience. If the product or the service is not intended for the specific audience who is looking for it, the marketing goes wrong.

So, to make the business reach the right audience and show the products to the people, it requires customer segmentation. With this project, we have planned to segment the customers based on their product searches, purchase history, and customer behavior. This makes the e-commerce platforms analyze the customer behavior towards the products, deals, etc. and they can target a particular area of customers during deals. Also, for the customers, it becomes hassle-free from all the unwanted or unperformable stuff.
